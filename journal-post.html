Creativity inspired by nature

We believe everything in life is inspired by mother nature

From our homes, to mobile phones, airplanes, fashion and even the footwear you put on, are inspired from natures creations. All creatures find ways to protect, store energy, insulate and feed themselves, alongside many others, including waste management. You only need to Google "designs inspired from nature", to discover a trov of stuffs mankind has learnt from nature.

At Fireside Africa we are no different; we infuse your design requests with inspirations we get from nature. The results are, for example, character designs and colors customized for you, your location and overall story; everyones story is unique. Growing up we all watched and have fond memories of cartoons we loved.

Being inspired by nature is not enough. We need to know the story...your story. What makes a bird fly, what type of bird is it, where doesn it live etc. These are question that when brought together, tell a story of the humming bird, for example. Whether you are a brand, startup or organization, we will transform your amazing word stories into beautiful animation, that resonates, excites and motivates...and increases your profits too. Let your unique story be heard.
